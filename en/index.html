What it does

Awareness at sea, in one simple tool

Most of Boreas is FREE. Features that need a subscription are marked PRO / PRO+; anything not built yet is marked PLANNED — nothing is oversold.

◎
Free

Offline nautical map

The Marmara chart ships with the app — no download, no internet. Depths, contours, soundings, lighthouses and navigation marks stay with you offshore.

➤
Free

Position · speed · course

Live position, speed (knots) and heading via GPS. It tells you when the signal is weak, and hides heading while you're stopped — because heading isn't reliable at a standstill.

↝
Free

Route planning & hazards

A suggested route that avoids land and shallows; drag the waypoints to edit it. Rocks, shallows and wrecks along your route are flagged in advance — thousands of hazard points derived from open-source bathymetry and wreck records. Requires the captain's verification.

!
Free

Safety alarms

The shallow-water alarm works from your own draft. Anchor watch catches dragging; MOB marks the spot with a single tap.

⌖
Free

Maritime boundary alerts

Warns you as you approach territorial waters. Internal waters, territorial waters and international waters are shown separately on the map — never cross a boundary unaware.

☼
Free

Weather & sea state

Wind, gusts, hourly forecast, waves, currents and sea level — powered by Open-Meteo data. It's a forecast, not a certainty. The latest data is stored offline and clearly marked when it goes stale.

✓
Free

Pre-departure check

One screen of judgment before you cast off: route, wind and changing weather. The forecast is taken from your route's departure point, not your last position. The wave & sea state line is part of Pro.

☾
Free

Night mode

A red-light scheme protects your night vision. Turn it on manually, or let it switch on by itself at sunset.

⚓
Free

Boat profile

Draft, cruising speed, fuel capacity and consumption — stored on your device. It calculates your range and tunes the shallow-water alarm to your boat.

◈
Free

Fleet — live position & messages

Share your position with the fleet if you choose, see nearby boats, wave hello, and message mutually accepted connections. Switch it off or block anyone, anytime.

✎
Free

Community notes + reporting

Leave a note on a cove: anchoring, shallow water, rocks, marina, fuel, fishing spot. Notes are marked as unverified user reports; flag inappropriate content with a single tap.

↺
Free

Voyage history

Every voyage is recorded automatically. Replay past trips on the map, see duration and distance, and share your voyage card.

◱
Free

3D harbor approach

Within 6 km of a harbor, its pre-built 3D approach card appears — see where you'll enter before you get there.

◇
Free

AIS vessel traffic

Live vessels around you (online, optional layer). For information only — not for collision avoidance.

⇩
Pro

Offline region packs

Aegean, Gökova-Bodrum, Mediterranean (West & East), Black Sea (West & East) and Cyprus. Download the region you're heading to and use it offshore without internet. Marmara is already free.

≋
Pro

Sea state in the departure check

Adds a wave height and sea state line to the pre-departure verdict — see the sea itself, not just the wind.

▲
Pro+

3D coastal view

See the coast from the sea: real terrain elevation (mountains rise) + up-to-date satellite imagery, with a live viewpoint on your device. Two cameras: deck and bird's-eye. Terrain awareness for docking and cove entries.

★
Planned

Verified community layer

Community observations verified over time — in a separate Boreas layer, never mixed into the base chart data.

◐
Planned

Photos

Cove and marina photos — after moderation and explicit consent, in the community layer.

Data principle

Community knowledge, kept apart from official data

Boreas keeps community observations separate from the open-source base chart data. User notes are never mixed into the OpenSeaMap/OSM-derived base chart. Observations whose accuracy is confirmed over time move to a separate "Verified Community Layer" — still an awareness source, not an official one.
